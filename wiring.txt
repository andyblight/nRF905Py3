This is how I wired up the nRF905 to my RPi 1.

Most wires go direct from nRF905 to RPi.  The 3 wires that need 1k resistors
go via a breadboard using a separate group of wires.

nRF905     Wire                        RPi pin
------     ----                        -------
VCC        Brown                       17
TxEN       Red                         10
CE         Orange                      13
PWR        Yellow                      15
UCK    NC
CD         Purple to breadboard        16
AM         White to breadboard         18
DR         Grey to breadboard          22
MISO       Purple                      21
MOSI       Blue                        19
SCK        White                       23
CSN        Grey                        24
GND        Black                       25
GND    NC
